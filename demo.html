(OCR) Demo Tutorial



This tutorial explains step by step on how to use our OCR technology to get the best of the experience.

1. To use our service, click on Home button on top-left of screen.
2. Enter URL of image or choose an image from your local device from which you want to convert the text.
3. The Selected Image will show up. Here you can edit your image as per your requirement.
4. After editing the image, click on Proceed and choose the language and the type of file in which you want to get the resulting text then click the OCR button and wait.
5. Click on Download button to download the file on your local device.
